# ilo_compensation
Analysis and visualization of workers hours and compensation using International Labour Organization data and R.

## 

1. This Rmarkdown, and pdf file of the final product, explores the relationship between weekly working hours and monetary compensation in European countries, according to the International Labour Organization (ILO).

2. Communicating with the tidyverse. They say that a picture is worth a thousand words. Successfully promoting your data analysis is not only a matter of accurate and effective graphics, but also of aesthetics and uniqueness.
